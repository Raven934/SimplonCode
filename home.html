Welcome to SimplonCode.

At SimplonCode, we see technology not merely as code, but as a canvas—where ideas become solutions, and curiosity sparks creation. Whether you're just beginning your journey or refining your craft, you've stepped into a vibrant community fueled by innovation, collaboration, and the thrill of hands-on discovery. Here, passionate mentors light the way, peers uplift and inspire, and every project is a stepping stone toward real-world impact. You won’t just learn to code—you’ll learn to think boldly, build fearlessly, and shape the future with every keystroke. So dive in, explore the unknown, and let your imagination lead. Your journey begins now—and we can’t wait to see the brilliance you’ll bring to life.

SimplonCode history begins in 2020 as a spark—an idea to make tech a path open to all. Born from the hearts of educators and technologists, it offers immersive bootcamps that turn beginners into builders, dreamers into developers. With hands-on learning, close-knit classes, and mentorship woven into every step, students gain not just skills but confidence. From underserved communities to global careers, alumni rise, create, and give back. SimplonCode’s soul lies in inclusion—welcoming women, minorities, and career changers into a space where creativity meets code. Whether online or on campus, its mission remains: to democratize technology, unlock potential, and craft a future shaped by diverse voices and bold ideas.

As the digital world continues to evolve, SimplonCode stands as a beacon—proving that talent knows no boundaries and opportunity should not be gated by privilege. Each graduate carries forward a ripple of change, building not just apps and systems, but communities and futures. With every cohort, the vision grows clearer: a tech industry enriched by diversity, driven by purpose, and powered by those who once only dreamed of belonging. SimplonCode isn’t just teaching code—it’s rewriting the narrative of who gets to shape tomorrow.

Why choose SimplonCode?

Choose SimplonCode because it’s more than a school it’s a launchpad for transformation. Here, code becomes a tool for possibility, and learning is fueled by purpose, passion, and people. With hands-on projects, close-knit classes, and mentorship at every turn, you’ll gain not just skills but the confidence to lead and create. SimplonCode welcomes your story, champions diversity, and empowers you to shape the future not alone, but together.

Our values

At SimplonCode, our values shape everything we do. We believe in empowerment—giving every learner the confidence and tools to lead, build, and innovate. We champion inclusivity, welcoming diverse stories and perspectives. Collaboration is at our core, with mentorship and teamwork driving shared success. Our learning is purpose-driven, rooted in real-world impact. And we pursue excellence through action, because transformation begins with bold steps.

Our pidagogy

At SimplonCode, our pedagogy is hands-on, human-centered, and purpose-driven. We believe the best way to learn is by doing—through real projects, teamwork, and solving meaningful challenges. Our small class sizes foster close mentorship and personalized support, while our inclusive approach ensures every learner feels seen, heard, and empowered. We don’t just teach code we teach confidence, collaboration, and the mindset to adapt, grow, and lead in a changing world.
